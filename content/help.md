# Help Center

## Saved Places

Saved places are currently stored locally on your device.

Future account systems will support syncing across devices.

## My Trip

You can add places and restaurants into your personal trip list for easier planning.

## AI Guide

HeriLand AI Guide combines local exploration data, trending places, and travel context to provide recommendations.

AI suggestions are designed to help discovery and should be used as travel references only.

## Place Information

Some opening hours, menus, prices, and location details may change over time.

Please confirm important information before visiting.

- Restaurant recommendations
- Feature suggestions
- Business collaborations
- Other feedback
