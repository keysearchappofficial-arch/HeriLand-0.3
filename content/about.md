# About HeriLand

## What is HeriLand?

HeriLand is a travel exploration platform focused on Sarawak.

The platform combines local places, restaurants, events, traveler stories, and AI-assisted recommendations into a slower and more immersive discovery experience.

## Our Vision

We believe travel is not only about visiting attractions.

Sometimes the most memorable experiences come from:

- everyday local food
- riverside walks
- hidden streets
- quiet cafés
- small conversations
- slow moments

HeriLand is designed to help people explore Sarawak beyond typical tourist routes.

## AI Guide

HeriLand AI Guide helps travelers discover places based on mood, interests, and local context.

The goal is not only navigation,
but helping travelers understand the atmosphere of a place.

## Community

Future versions of HeriLand may include:

- traveler accounts
- saved trip syncing
- community reviews
- personalized recommendations
- local business partnerships

## Platform Status

HeriLand is currently in active development.

Some features, information, and recommendations may continue to improve over time.
